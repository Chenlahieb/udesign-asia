About UDesign.Asie

Built in Siem Reap, for Siem Reap.

UDesign.Asie is an architecture and construction studio based in Siem Reap, Cambodia. We work with homeowners, hospitality operators, and businesses who want buildings designed around how this region actually lives, its heat, its light, its materials, and its pace.

Founded by TOLA Hieb, the studio pairs architectural design with in-house construction management, so every project is delivered by the same team that designed it, no handoffs, no lost intent.

  • 01

    Site-first design

    Every drawing starts from the plot, its orientation, and its climate, not a template.

  • 02

    One team, start to finish

    Design and construction sit under the same roof, so intent survives the build.

  • 03

    Honest materials

    We favor materials that age well in this climate, concrete, laterite, timber, local stone.
